Returns & Refunds

Understanding our return policy for artwork purchases.

Our Return Policy

At FL Sculptures, we understand that purchasing artwork is a significant decision. Each sculpture is a unique, handcrafted piece created with care and attention to detail. Due to the unique nature of our artwork, we have established the following return policy:

No Standard Returns

Because each sculpture is a one-of-a-kind artwork, we do not accept standard returns. We encourage all potential buyers to:

  • Request detailed photos and videos of the artwork before purchasing
  • Schedule a studio visit to view the piece in person (when possible)
  • Ask any questions about materials, dimensions, or condition
  • Review the certificate of authenticity and provenance documentation

Damage During Shipping

If your sculpture arrives damaged due to shipping, we will work with you to resolve the issue. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will either:

  • Arrange for professional restoration at our expense
  • Provide a replacement if a similar piece is available
  • Offer a full refund in rare cases of irreparable damage

Material Defects

In the unlikely event of a material defect or structural issue not disclosed prior to sale, please contact us within 30 days of delivery. We will assess the situation and work toward a fair resolution, which may include repair, replacement, or refund.

Custom Commissions

For custom-commissioned pieces, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to begin work. Once work has begun, cancellations are not accepted. The final 50% payment is due upon completion and before delivery. No returns on custom commissions.

Refund Process

In cases where a refund is approved (shipping damage, material defects), refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
