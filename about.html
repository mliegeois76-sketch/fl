About

The Artist

A sculptural approach between tradition and contemporaneity.

FL is a contemporary sculptor whose work explores the boundaries between abstraction and figuration. Each piece is the result of constant research into balance, matter, and space.

Trained at the Beaux-Arts de Paris, FL has developed a personal technique blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary innovation. Their work has been exhibited in several European galleries and is part of international private collections.

Philosophy

The principles that guide my creation.

01

Matter

Bronze, stone, and wood are my preferred materials. Each material dictates its own language and imposes its creative constraints.

02

Balance

The search for perfect balance between full and empty, mass and lightness, is at the heart of each sculpture.

03

Time

Each work carries within it the time of its creation. The traces of manual work are visible, witnesses to patience and precision.

04

Emotion

Art must move. My sculptures seek to evoke an emotional response, a silent connection with the viewer.
